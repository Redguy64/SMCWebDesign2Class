About Us

Who Are We

Neighborhood hardware store specializing in key services, Valspar paint, custom screen and glass services and plumbing fixtures and tools. Peterson’s Hardware Store has been part of the community since 1958. We are here to help you with any hardware, plumbing, and household needs. We also repair windows,screens, cut glass, mix paints, and make keys. As a mom and pop store we are grateful for your business and are open 7 days a week for whatever you need during these hard times.
